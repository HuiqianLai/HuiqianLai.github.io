About Me

Hello! I'm Mia Huiqian Lai, a PhD student at Syracuse University in Information Science and Technology, where my research focuses on misinformation, disinformation, and computational communication.

My academic journey has been shaped by a strong passion for artificial intelligence and digital media, which I explored during my MA in Communication at Hong Kong Baptist University. With a solid foundation in software engineering from Shandong Jianzhu University and further technical experience through an exchange program in Taiwan, I've developed a deep expertise in data analytics, AI, and machine learning.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I bring extensive experience as a Senior Android Development Engineer, having worked at companies like WPS, NetEase Games, and Qihoo 360. Over the years, I have led projects in app development, mobile UI design, and machine learning, enhancing the functionality and user experience of various products. I am very good at mobile development and its dynamics, and understand its principles (webview, luaview, flutter, plugin, etc.) My industry experience has allowed me to apply my technical skills in real-world settings, particularly in mobile development and AI-driven solutions.

Outside of my professional and academic life, I am passionate about exploring the intersection of AI and communication, and I am constantly seeking ways to apply these technologies to solve societal challenges. In my free time, I enjoy staying connected with the tech community through conferences, research collaborations, and hands-on projects. I look forward to share my journey, research, and insights with you!
